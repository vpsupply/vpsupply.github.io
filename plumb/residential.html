Residential Bath

Comfort, safety, style, convenience. These are important aspects to consider when refining your home. The way you care for your family and guests is poured into the choices you make for your bathroom. Choices like materials, componentry, features and efficiencies. You can trust Innovations to help you create your vision of the ideal bathroom. Innovations by VP; a division of VP Supply Corp, is your residential kitchen and bath design resource. Innovations has 9 unique showrooms located in Rochester, Buffalo, Albany, Plattsburgh, Syracuse, Ballston Lake, Vestal, New Hartford, New York and Erie, Pennsylvania. Each showcasing product displays of the latest plumbing and bath products, as well as an extensive product selection of reputable brands such as Moen, Delta, and American Standard. Imagine the possibilities!
