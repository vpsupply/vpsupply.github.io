24-Hour Water Heater Replacement Program

When water is involved, property owners have to act fast or the repair, clean up and replacement costs can get extreme. That is where we come in. Our emergency replacement program means you are one phone call away from a solution for your customer. A VP representative is always available for either pick up or emergency delivery of a replacement commercial water heater.
If the emergency is during normal business hours, please contact your local branch directly first! Otherwise, please call our emergency number: 1-866-666-3762. There is a $75 call out fee for the usage of this service. Delivery and haul-off fees apply. All information is available from any sales representative from any VP Supply branch location.
